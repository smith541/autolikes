What Are Instagram Monthly Likes?

Instagram Monthly Likes refer to the total number of likes your posts receive over a one-month period. Whether you're sharing photos, videos, or reels, every like contributes to your monthly total. Instagram does not explicitly show a "monthly likes" section, but users can calculate these totals by analyzing their post history. For businesses and creators, this metric serves as a useful tool to understand content performance trends over time.

By analyzing Monthly Likes, you can adjust your content strategy to focus on posts that receive the highest engagement. For instance, if your audience prefers video content over static photos, you can begin creating more videos to maintain or boost engagement.

3.Track Engagement Growth

For influencers and brands, monitoring the rise or fall in likes month by month can reveal growth patterns. An increase in Monthly Likes could indicate that your content is reaching a larger audience, while a drop may suggest the need to adjust your posting schedule or content style.

4. Understand Audience Behavior

Monthly Likes data can also reveal valuable insights into audience behavior. By noting the times or days your posts receive the most likes, you can tailor your posting schedule to maximize reach and engagement.
